Free Home Maintenance for the People Who Serve Our Communities

Every project starts with a family that needs help and a team of student volunteers ready to deliver it. We don't write checks or make referrals — we show up with tools, materials, and the energy to get the job done.

Home Repairs

Leaking faucets. Broken steps. A porch railing that's been loose for two years. These are the projects that pile up when life is spent serving others. Our volunteer crews handle basic home repairs — plumbing fixes, carpentry, painting, weatherproofing, accessibility modifications — so the people who protect and teach our communities can live in homes that work.

Typical Projects

Interior and exterior painting, minor plumbing, fence repair, door and window fixes, ramp installation, handrail repair.

Yard Cleanup

An overgrown yard isn't just an eyesore — it's a signal. It tells a neighbor that someone inside is struggling. Our crews clear, mow, trim, haul, and restore outdoor spaces so that the home looks as cared-for as the person living in it deserves.

Typical Projects

Lawn mowing, hedge trimming, leaf removal, brush clearing, garden bed restoration, mulching.

Snow Removal

When Central Virginia gets hit with winter weather, driveways and walkways become barriers — especially for older veterans and first responders recovering from injuries. Our teams clear snow and ice so no one is trapped inside their own home.

Typical Projects

Driveway clearing, sidewalk shoveling, porch and step de-icing, pathway salting.

Relocation Assistance

Military families move more than most. Teachers relocate for new positions. First responders transfer between departments. Moving is expensive and exhausting — and it's worse when you're doing it alone. We provide volunteer crews to help with packing, loading, unloading, and setting up the new space.

Typical Projects

Packing assistance, loading and unloading, furniture arrangement, unpacking support.

Pet & Animal Care

When a service member is deployed or a first responder is recovering, the family pet still needs to be walked, fed, and looked after. Our volunteers help with basic animal care so families can focus on what matters most without worrying about their four-legged family members.

Typical Projects

Dog walking, feeding routines, basic grooming, yard cleanup for pet areas.

Waste Disposal

Years of deferred maintenance leave behind more than broken fixtures — they leave piles of debris, old furniture, and accumulated junk that a single family can't haul alone. Our crews handle the heavy lifting: sorting, loading, hauling, and disposing of waste so the home is clean, safe, and functional.

Typical Projects

Junk removal, debris hauling, garage cleanout, shed clearing, post-renovation cleanup.
