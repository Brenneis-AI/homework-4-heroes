You've Served Your Community. Let Us Take Care of Your Home.

Asking for Help Is Not a Weakness. It's a Next Step.

You've spent years giving to this community — running into emergencies, standing in front of classrooms, serving overseas. The house projects piling up aren't a failure. They're the natural cost of a life spent focused on something bigger than yourself. HomeWork 4 Heroes exists so you don't carry that weight alone.

Eligibility

Who We Serve

HomeWork 4 Heroes provides free home maintenance and support services to the following groups in Central Virginia:

military_tech

Veterans & Military Families

Active duty, retired, or honorably discharged service members and their immediate families.

local_fire_department

First Responders

Firefighters, EMTs, paramedics, law enforcement officers, and their immediate families.

school

Teachers & Educators

Current public and private school teachers, instructional staff, and their immediate families.

Service Area: Central Virginia — Charlottesville, Albemarle, Fluvanna, Louisa, Greene, and Nelson counties.

If you're unsure whether you qualify, reach out. We'd rather answer a question than miss someone who needs help.

The Process

Three Steps to Getting Help

1

Submit a Request

Fill out the form below with your name, contact info, and a description of what you need. Photos help us plan, but they're not required.

2

We Review & Match

Our team reviews your request, confirms eligibility, and matches you with a volunteer crew that fits the project.

3

Volunteers Show Up

A student team arrives on the scheduled date with tools and materials. You don't pay a dime. You don't lift a finger unless you want to.

Common Questions

Is this really free?

Yes. HomeWork 4 Heroes is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All services are provided at no cost to eligible families. Our projects are funded by donations and community sponsors.

How long does a project take?

Most projects are completed in a single day. Larger or multi-phase projects may require additional visits, which we'll coordinate with you in advance.

What if I need help again later?

You can submit a new request anytime. Repeat requests are welcome — our goal is sustained support, not a one-time visit.

Do I need to be home during the project?

We prefer it, but we understand schedules. We'll work out the details when we confirm your project date.

What if my project doesn't fit your categories?

Submit a request anyway and describe what you need. If we can't help directly, we'll try to connect you with someone who can.

You Don't Have to Do This Alone

Asking for help after a lifetime of giving it isn't easy. We know that. HomeWork 4 Heroes was founded by military dependents who watched their own family navigate that tension. This organization exists because no one who serves should have to struggle with the basics alone.

If you'd rather talk to a person before filling out a form, call us at (434) 555-0147 or email info@homework4heroes.org. We're here.
