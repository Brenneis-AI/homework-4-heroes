Student Ambassadors

Lead the Mission at Your School

Student Ambassadors are the backbone of HomeWork 4 Heroes. They recruit volunteers from their schools, help coordinate project logistics, and represent the organization at campus events and community gatherings. It's not a title — it's a responsibility, and it's one of the most meaningful leadership roles a high school or college student can hold.

What Ambassadors Do:

  • Recruit and organize volunteer teams at their school
  • Help coordinate project day logistics
  • Represent HW4H at school events and community functions
  • Serve as the point of contact between their school and the organization

How to Become an Ambassador:

Sign up as a volunteer first. After completing 3 projects, you're eligible to apply for the Ambassador program. Current Ambassadors and the HW4H leadership team select new Ambassadors each semester.
